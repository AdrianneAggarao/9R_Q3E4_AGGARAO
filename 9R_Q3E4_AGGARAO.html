Titans in Attack on Titan

Titan Picture Titan Name Titan's Person Description
War Hammer Titan Lara Tybur Structures consisting of hardened Titan flesh might be created and manipulated by the War Hammer Titan. These structures may be turned into weapons, such as war hammers, spikes big enough to lift and impale a 15-meter Titan, and spike floods that would cover entire territories.
Jaw Titan Porco Galliard An immensely satisfying coming-of-age story wrapped around an intriguing adventure. Affluent, popular and athletic, high school junior Jack Morrison had everything, and everything to lose. His life begins to derail when his mother dies of cancer.
Beast Titan Zeke Yeager The destructive ability to toss things with incredible accuracy and destructive power is possessed by the Beast Titan. During the Battle of Shiganshina, Zeke Jaeger's Beast Titan famously used this to destroy the majority of the Scout Regiment warriors.
Colossal Titan Armin Arlert The Colossal Titan, as its name suggests, is known for its enormous height. It stands at a previously unheard-of 60 meters, high enough to look over the top of the Walls. Despite its enormous size and overpowering physical strength, its movements are extremely slow because to its massive body mass.
Attack Titan Eren Yeager The Attack Titan was special in that those who inherited it were able to receive both past and present-day memories. The Attack Titan's user had some degree of access to a future inheritor's memories as well as the ability to witness events that their future selves would go through.
